Dunder Mifflin Management System

[ Home | Customers | Employees | Branches | Departments | Invoices | Invoice Items | Products]

This database management system is designed for the use of Dunder Mifflin employees to manage Customers, Sales, and Products effectively. The following tables may be found within the database:

Customers contains customer contact information, and information on the customer's branch and Dunder Mifflin sales representative.

Employees: contains employee contact, department, and branch information.

Branches: contains each Dunder Mifflin branch, with each branche's address and main phone number.

Departments: a list of departments within Dunder Mifflin.

Invoices: a record of each sale completed, with customer and employee information included.

Invoice Items: maintains a record of each product included on a particular invoice.

Products: contains information about the products sold by Dunder Mifflin.
